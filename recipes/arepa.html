Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of Water
  • 1/3 cup of Harina Pan
  • Pinch of Salt
  • 2 slices of Cheese
  • Some Butter
  • Protein of choice
  • Cup of Coffee

Steps

  1. Pour water in bowl and mix with Harina Pan
  2. Keep mixing until smooth consistency
  3. Butter the stove
  4. Make balls with mixture, flatten them, and lay them on the stove
  5. Flip arepas until cooked
  6. Serve the arepas, and stuff them with cheese and your protein and drink the coffee
