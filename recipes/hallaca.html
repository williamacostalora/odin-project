Hallaca

Ingredients

  • Meat Guiso
  • Olives
  • Raisins
  • Bellpepper
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Harina Pan
  • Anatto
  • Eggs
  • Plantain leaves

Steps

  1. Make the meat guiso of your choice
  2. Cook the eggs
  3. Make the anatto micture and pour it in the Harina Pan
  4. Make balls with Harina Pan and flatten them out and stuff the guiso and the eggs and the cut peppers
  5. Cover with plantain leaves
  6. Boil for 30 minutes and let rest before eating
